### AI BOM Research Paper (arXiv)

**Academic Publication**

A detailed research paper presenting the theoretical foundations and practical implementations of AI Bill of Materials using SPDX standards.

**[Read on arXiv :material-arrow-right:](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.07070){ .md-button .md-button--primary }**

**Abstract:**

This paper explores AI Bill of Materials as a mechanism for improving AI system transparency, reproducibility, and accountability. It presents SPDX-based approaches for documenting AI models, datasets, and their dependencies.

**Key Contributions:**

- Formal framework for AI BOM
- SPDX AI and Dataset profile specifications
- Case studies and implementations
- Security and compliance considerations
- Future directions for AI documentation standards
