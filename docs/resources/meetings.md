# Meeting Minutes

Stay updated with the latest discussions and decisions from our working group meetings.

## Regular Meetings

The SPDX AI Working Group holds regular meetings to discuss progress, review proposals, and coordinate activities. All meeting minutes are publicly available for transparency and community engagement.

## Access Meeting Minutes

Our meeting minutes, agendas, and recordings are maintained in the SPDX Meetings repository:

**[View Meeting Minutes :material-arrow-right:](https://github.com/spdx/meetings/tree/main/ai){ .md-button .md-button--primary }**

The meeting notes include:

- Meeting agendas and topics
- Discussion summaries
- Action items and decisions
- Attendance records
- Links to recordings (when available)

## Meeting Schedule

For information about upcoming meetings and how to join, please check:

- The [SPDX Meetings repository](https://github.com/spdx/meetings/tree/main/ai)
- The [SPDX mailing lists](https://lists.spdx.org/mailman/listinfo)
- The [SPDX Community Calendar](https://www.spdx.dev/participate/meetings/)

## How to Participate

We welcome participation from all interested parties:

1. **Join our meetings** - Check the schedule and dial-in details in the meetings repository
2. **Review meeting minutes** - Stay informed about recent discussions and decisions
3. **Contribute to discussions** - Share your expertise and perspectives
4. **Submit proposals** - Help shape the development of AI and Dataset profiles

## Contact

For questions about meetings or to get added to the meeting invitations, please reach out through:

- SPDX mailing lists
- GitHub discussions in the [spdx-ai repository](https://github.com/spdx-ai/spdx-ai)
- SPDX Slack workspace
